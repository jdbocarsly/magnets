The magnet database
The magnet database contains experimental and calculated (density functional theory) data for 167 transition metal-based ferromagnets (including 33 magnetocalorics). The apps on the website are designed to showcase trends and correlations for data-driven discovery.

For more information, see:
J.D. Bocarsly, E.E. Levin, C.A.C. Garcia, K. Schwennicke, S.D. Wilson, R. Seshadri, A Simple Computational Proxy for Screening Magnetocaloric Compounds, Chem. Mater. 29 (2017) 1613-1622. [doi]

The website is under active development. Please feel free to contact us with any questions, comments, and feature requests.
